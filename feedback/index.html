Feedback & Contact

WATcalendars is an ongoing project that constantly evolves to accourately refflect real class schedules and make them easy to follow.
Despite my best efforts, there may be issues - for example, a schedule for a specific group might display incorrectly, classes might shift or overlap, or certain elements may not appear as expected.

To keep the calendars accurate and reliable, I'm relying on your feedback.

If you notice any errors in your group's schedule or have suggestions for improvements, please let me know using one of the methods listed below.

E-mail

You can contact me directly via email at serafin652002@gmail.com,
or send a message using your preferred email client.

serafin652002@gmail.com



In your message, please include:

  • Short title/issue or improvements description,
  • Date, when the issue occurred,
  • (Optional) screenshots or examples that might help.

Reporting Issues & Suggestions

You can also report issues or suggest improvements directly from Github - it's the fastest way for me to respond and track progress.

Also, on the github page, you can find contributing guidelines if you want to help improve the project by yourself - check it out.

!Important

Please avoid sharing any personal or sensitive information in your messages. I respect your privacy and will only use the information you provide to address the reported issues or suggestions.
